Cybersecurity

Healthcare cybersecurity is related to the use of the internet and computers. If you don’t have security on your mobile device or computer, you could be leaving a trail of information that hackers can find and exploit. It is important to use safe internet practices because it’s not just your physical health that can be affected by internet use—it’s your personal information that can also be compromised.

Behavior

Before sharing any personal information, ask yourself a few important questions. For example: Is this my bank's official website? If it isn’t, and the site is asking for your banking details, it’s not a good idea to share that information. There are many software firewalls and identity protection services available for personal and business use. Some companies that offer cybersecurity solutions include Norton. Other companies that help protect information include LifeLock and Iron Mountain.
