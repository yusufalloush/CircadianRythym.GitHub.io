Solutions That Need to Be Discussed

Since blue light inhibits sleep by suppressing melatonin (the body's natural sleep hormone), blue light-blocking glasses have been developed. These glasses help block blue light, and can be especially useful for older phones that don't have a screen that filters blue light. Many new phones now come with built-in blue light filters. Since when did "sitting too close to the TV" become okay? Are we willing to risk our health for the sake of technology? Future phones may emit a different type of light. If cellphone screens were designed to filter light from the outset, similar to how the ozone layer protects the Earth from the sun's heat, we could have safer smartphones. Future developments in chemical engineering may hold the key to safer phones.
